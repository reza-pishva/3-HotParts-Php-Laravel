People entry and exit management software in industrial centers

Due to the importance of monitoring the entry and exit of people in industrial centers, it is always necessary to properly manage the entry and exit of people who enter these centers for various reasons. Due to the importance of monitoring the entry and exit of people in industrial centers, it is always necessary to properly manage the entry and exit of people who enter these centers for various reasons. These people may want to enter the power plant for various reasons. For example, there are always some students who apply to the power plant for an internship. In many cases, in order to participate in periodic repairs or special repairs, it is necessary for expert groups from different companies to enter the power plant

These devices that these people bring into the power plant may interfere with the operation of control systems in different parts of the power plant, so the entry of these devices into the power plant should be accompanied by careful monitoring, for example, if the presence of these people requires some points. It should be safe before the presence of these people Safety items in the application forms of the presence of these people to be specified so that before the start of the activity of these people in the power plant to be trained by the safety unit. In addition to the period of time when these people are allowed to be present at the power plant, the places where they can be present must also be known in advance to the power plant officials.

Given the above, the existence of software that can cover the needs of industrial units seemed necessary. In the management software of people entering and leaving the power plant, it is possible for each part of the power plant to fill out a request form for the presence of people from outside the power plant, and this request form is sent to these departments in a fully mechanized manner for approval by managers of different departments. After obtaining the necessary approvals, the person or persons will be allowed to enter the power plant. Through this software, it is possible to register the reason for the presence of this person and the company that works there, along with other details such as phone number and email address. There are others that can be very useful for future reference to these people or companies. This software includes various sections such as various reports and also forms to determine the names of people and tools and cars and so on.In this sofware project I have used php and its framework laravel in server side and in client side I have used HTML,CSS,Bootstrap,Javascript,jQuery and Ajax techniques. Database that I have used in this project was mySql.
