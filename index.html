Education management system

In this software I have provided the possibility to evaluate the improvement of students by different reports in a way we can compare the educational condition of each student with others with radar charts , bar charts, and line charts. This sofware is provided with Vue.js as frontend and php/laravel as backend and to present graphic reports I have used Charts.js. This software have three main parts. The first one is used to classified students in different classes and majors and we can define lessons for each class and assign them to each student. In this sofware we can register different scores for students and we can provide CRUD operations for each table.

In the following, two other types of graphic reporting can be seen to compare the academic status of a student with other students in the class. In the first report, the bar graph of the average scores of different education courses can be seen along with the class average of that course. In the second diagram of the radar chart, the student's average scores in different subjects are compared with other classmates.

In the next part you can see the form used to assign classes to students and we can easily change our decision and assign them to another class.
