In many industrial centers and power plants, there are parts that work in very high temperature conditions and therefore are made of expensive alloys, and therefore, for the optimal use of these parts, it is necessary to have a good history of preparation and maintenance of these parts. And referred to them for preventive repairs. These parts may be used in different parts of the plant at different intervals, all of which must be recorded as part history. In addition, the date that this piece was sent outside the industrial unit for reconstruction and the number of hours it worked at that time are very important

Any repairs that are done on these parts or when it is sent to the warehouse of an industrial unit that will be used again later will help us to use these parts optimally and will definitely increase the life of these parts.

In addition, keeping records of these parts helps us to identify the right time to decommission these parts, and in this case, we will prevent dangerous and costly accidents in industrial units. In this software, an attempt has been made to define programs for each piece, which will later be attributed to each piece as a record.These programs can include items such as entering and leaving the warehouse, periodic repairs, sending to reconstruction, and other items. If these programs are assigned to a group of parts, it will be possible to define groups and then place parts that have the same function in one group, and consider different repair programs together for parts that are in the same group. Be taken and finally shape the history of these pieces

In this software, features such as moving parts between different groups and creating a new group are considered. This software has a variety of reports in designing a schedule to send parts for reconstruction or decommissioning parts based on the number of hours they have worked and the number of times they have been sent out of the power plant for reconstruction. It helps a lot. This software has been very popular with the owners of the power plant and currently only the Kazerun power plant is used and has no analogues in the power plant industry. Therefore, requests to purchase this software have been sent by other power plants.In this sofware project I have used php and its framework laravel in server side and in client side I have used HTML,CSS,Bootstrap,Javascript,jQuery and Ajax techniques. Database that I have used in this project was mySql
