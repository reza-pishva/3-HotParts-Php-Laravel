Power plant equipment entry and exit management software

In all industrial centers, for a variety of reasons, it is necessary to remove parts from the power plant and return to that place. For example, some of these parts are taken out of the power plant for reconstruction or repair. In addition, it is necessary to closely monitor the time of entry and exit of parts to make it possible to plan for periodic repairs as well as preventive repairs.

In addition, in case of any of these parts that leave the power plant, it is necessary to determine the person who is responsible for following up the issues related to that part. Enjoys. In the software designed to manage the entry and exit of parts, there are forms that specify the details of the part and the place to be transferred and the person who is in charge of following the issues related to this part and also has different stations including request The contractor is directly responsible for the protection of the power plant and the manager of the power plant, and access levels have been applied to various stations.

Due to the role that this software has for planning power plant repairs and the positive impact that it has on preventive repairs, this software was welcomed by power plant owners. Also, after the introduction of this software in a meeting attended by security managers of various power plants, requests were sent to use this software in other industrial environments.In this sofware project I have used php and its framework laravel in server side and in client side I have used HTML,CSS,Bootstrap,Javascript,jQuery and Ajax techniques. Database that I have used in this project was mySql.
